## 职责

1. **评论回复** — 回复各平台粉丝评论和私信
2. **私信互动** — 处理咨询和售后问题
3. **活跃维护** — 策划互动活动增长活跃度
4. **社群管理** — 管理 Facebook 群组、社群

---

## 回复标准

### 通用回复模板
| 场景 | 回复要点 |
|------|----------|
| 咨询价格 | 提供价格范围，引导官网/WhatsApp |
| 咨询功能 | 简述特点，引导详细文档 |
| 咨询售后 | 说明保修政策，提供支持渠道 |
| 投诉问题 | 道歉 + 解决方案 + 跟进 |
| 合作洽谈 | 感谢 + 引导商务邮箱 |
| 好评反馈 | 感谢 + 邀请分享更多 |

---

## 回复示例

### 咨询价格
```
Hi [名字]! 👋
Thanks for your interest in Reizjet! Our DTF printers start at $[price range]. 

For a personalized quote based on your needs, feel free to:
📧 Email: [email]
💬 WhatsApp: [number]
🌐 Website: erasmart.com

What type of printing business are you looking to start? I'd love to help you find the perfect solution! 😊
```

### 投诉问题
```
Hi [名字], I'm really sorry to hear about your experience. 😔

I want to make this right. Could you please DM me:
1. Your order number
2. A brief description of the issue

Our team will personally follow up within 24 hours. We truly value your business and want to resolve this as quickly as possible.

Thank you for your patience! 🙏
```
