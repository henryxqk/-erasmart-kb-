## ⚠️ Risk Management

### Identified Risks
| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Algorithm changes | High | Medium | Diversify platforms |
| Supply chain issues | Medium | High | Safety stock, communication |
| Competitor pricing | High | High | Emphasize quality/service |
| Influencer fraud | Medium | Medium | Vet with engagement rates |
| Currency fluctuations | Medium | Low | Local pricing where possible |
| Shipping delays | Medium | High | US warehouse communication |

### Contingency Plans
1. **Ad account ban** → Diversify ad accounts, use multiple platforms
2. **Influencer scandal** → Vet thoroughly, contract clauses
3. **Product launch delay** → Pre-built content calendar
4. **Economic downturn** → Emphasize ROI, financing options
